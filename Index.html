Hello there, I'm Shashikant Sharma, but you can call me Shashi! Originally from Bihar, I currently reside in Delhi, India. I have a passion for reading and enjoy immersing myself in the world of literature through audiobooks and podcasts. When I'm not indulging in a good book, you can find me cycling, walking, or even climbing to stay active and connected with nature.

One thing that truly defines me is my eclectic taste in music. From pop and 80s/90s Hindi classics to Punjabi beats, I can groove to just about anything. Some of my favorite artists include Chainsmokers, Weeknd, and Daljeet Dosangh. When it comes to movies, I find myself drawn to thought-provoking ones like "Tamasha" and "Rockstar."

Creating a strong online presence and showcasing my work are my goals for my personal website. It's a platform where I hope to connect with others and share my passion for design and various interests. I had the privilege to participate in an Expo at IIT Kanpur and enjoyed exploring the beauty of Jim Corbett National Park and Sariska National Park.
